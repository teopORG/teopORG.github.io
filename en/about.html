About Us

History and Background

teopORG was founded in 2025 as a non-profit organization focused on educational poverty alleviation. We are committed to helping children in poor areas change their destiny through education, giving them the opportunity to receive better education and realize their dreams.

Mission and Vision

Our mission is: to help children in poor areas of Nigeria change their destiny through education.

Our vision is: to ensure that every child has equal access to education, regardless of where they are born.

Our Team

Our team consists of a group of caring and responsible volunteers, including educators, social workers, and entrepreneurs. We work together to provide better educational resources and opportunities for children in poor areas.

Donation Appeal

Every donation can directly help children in need. Your support will provide them with better educational opportunities and change their future.
